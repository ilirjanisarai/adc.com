Program Overview

We’re excited to announce Microsoft’s “Operator CSP” program, developed for telecom partners like Vodafone. This is a major opportunity for us to drive even more value for our customers. Through this program, Vodafone can offer Microsoft 365 licenses at exclusive, discounted rates, reinforcing our competitive advantage and enabling us to deliver more digital solutions.

This offer is not just a pricing advantage, it's a strategic step forward allowing us to bundle best-in-class productivity tools with our core connectivity and digital services.
